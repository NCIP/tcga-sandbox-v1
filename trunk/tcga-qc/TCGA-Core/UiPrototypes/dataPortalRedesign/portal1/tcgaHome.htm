Welcome to The Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA) Data Portal.
The TCGA Data Portal provides a platform for researchers to search, download, and analyze data sets generated by TCGA. This portal contains all TCGA data pertaining to clinical information associated with cancer tumors and human subjects, genomic characterization, and high-throughput sequencing analysis of the tumor genomes.
