NCICB Cancer Molecular Analysis (CMA) Portal The Cancer Molecular Analysis Portal (CMA), a component of the National Cancer Institute’s caBIG™ initiative, is designed to enable researchers to integrate, visualize, and explore clinical and genomic characterization data from translational research studies, such as The Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA) Pilot Project. Any (browser based)
NCICB Cancer Genome Workbench (CGWB) The Cancer Genome Workbench is a viewer for integrated cancer genomic data. Any (browser based)
Broad Institute Integrative Genomics Viewer (IGV) The Integrative Genomics Viewer (IGV) is a fast, flexible viewer for genomic data. IGV visually integrates datasets from various platforms and sources, including genetic variation data, gene/microarray expression data epigenetic data, RNAi screens, genomic features and clinical and phenotype annoation of samples. Any (Java Based)
MSKCC MSKCC CancerGenomics Analysis This site presents the results of detailed analysis and access to analysis tools in the areas of signaling pathways, mutations, and copy number alterations. Any (Java Based)
