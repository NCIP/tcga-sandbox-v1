Sorting the List
By default, the table is sorted alphabetically by pathway. To sort the table by another column, follow these steps:
1.
Click a column heading, and an up arrow appears next to the column name.
All pathways with a black dot in that column are moved to the top. For example, in the following figure when the Mutations arrow points up, the pathways with mutations move to the top.
2.
Click the column heading again, and a down arrow appears next to the column name.
All pathways with a black dot in that column are moved to the bottom.
3.
Click the column name to hide the arrow, and the table returns to being sorted alphabetically by pathway.
4.
To move all pathways with an anomaly to the top, click the Any Anomaly/Agent column name until the up arrow displays.
5.
To move pathways without anomalies to the top, click the Any Anomaly/Agent column name until the down arrow displays.
