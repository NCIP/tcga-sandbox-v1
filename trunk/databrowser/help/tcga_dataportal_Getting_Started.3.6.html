Determining Mutations
The mutation data in the TCGA Data Portal Prototype protype originated from 61 tumor samples from Broad Institute at MIT ( WIBR file) and 28 tumor samples from Baylor (BCM file).
Due to the complexity of the mechanism of cancer, not all genes involved in the production of the tumor will necessarily have a mutation.
