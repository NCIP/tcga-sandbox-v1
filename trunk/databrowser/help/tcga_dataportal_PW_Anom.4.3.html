Displaying and Navigating the List
To display more items on one page or navigate to items in the list, follow these steps:
1.
To display a longer list of items on one page, click the Rows Displayed list box in the upper right-hand corner. Select the number of items to display at once: 15, 50, or 100.
2.
To move to another page of the list, click one of the arrow buttons next to the Rows Displayed list, You can move directly to the first and last page or page to the previous or next page.
