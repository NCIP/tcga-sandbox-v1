Reviewing Genes with Potential Abnormalities
The Pathway Gene Anomalies table lists the genes for the selected pathway and the anomalies, mutations, and agents associated with each gene.
1.
To access this page, select a pathway on the Viewing Pathways and Associate Abnormalities page. The BioCarta pathway diagram appears at the top of the page.
2.
To display the Pathway Gene Anomalies table, scroll to the bottom of the page .
The Pathway Gene Anomalies table lists the genes for the selected pathway and indicates with a black dot anomalies associated with each gene. Any agents associated with the gene are named.
Caution:
Do not use the Back button in your browser to return to the pathway list. Scroll to the top of the page and click Return to Pathway List.
3.
To display detailed information about genes and agents, follow these steps:
1.
For detailed gene information, click the gene name hyperlink.
A window opens to the NCBI Entrez Gene page with detailed information about the selected gene.
2.
For detailed agent information, click the agent name hyperlink.
A window opens to the NCI Thesaurus page with detailed information about the selected agent.
3.
On the Pathway Gene Anomalies table, you can perform the following tasks:
•
Displaying and Navigating the List
•
Sorting the List
•
Highlighting Column Data on the BioCarta Diagram
