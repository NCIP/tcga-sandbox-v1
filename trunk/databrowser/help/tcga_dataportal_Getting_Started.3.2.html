About TCGA Data Portal Prototype
The data on this site is preliminary and intended only to demonstrate the functions of this Alpha release. Use the data at your own risk. This data is subject to change. We welcome your feedback on this release and its associated data. Please email us at ncicb@pop.nci.nih.gov with questions or comments.
The Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA) Data Portal prototype currently under development analyzes data and categorizes genes into the appropriate anomaly category. The following list describes the different categories:
•
For copy number data, TCGA Data Portal Prototype categorizes genes into the Amplified or Deleted anomaly categories.
•
Mutated genes are categorized into the Mutated anomaly category.
•
The TCGA Data Portal Prototype also indicates any Agent (drug) data associated with the gene.
