Highlighting Column Data on the BioCarta Diagram
To highlight on the BioCarta diagram the genes associated with a certain column of data, click the column heading. For example, to highlight the mutated genes on the Biocarta diagram, click the Mutations column heading. The mutated genes are then highlighted on the diagram.
