Viewing a Pathway Map and Gene Anomalies
The data on this site is preliminary and intended only to demonstrate the functions of this Alpha release. Use the data at your own risk. This data is subject to change. We welcome your feedback on this release and its associated data. Please email us at ncicb@pop.nci.nih.gov with questions or comments.
After you select a pathway on the Pathways and Associated Abnormalities page:
•
The top of the page is a map of the selected biochemical pathway projected onto cell morphology (See Viewing a Pathway Map onto the Cell Morphology)
•
The Pathway Genes Anomalies table at bottom of the page lists the genes for the selected pathway and the anomalies associated with each gene (Reviewing Genes with Potential Abnormalities)
